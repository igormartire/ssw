About us

I have been an equestrian for over two decades. I became tired of using toxic, dangerous and environmentally harmful fly sprays on my horses. Using my background in essential oils and holistic treatments, I created Shelley Says! Fly Away!, a non-toxic, all-natural, and unique fly spray. It’s not only good for your horse—it’s good for you, the rider. It’s made with love and kindness from one horse lover to another.

All natural, made with five essential oils; citronella, lemon grass, peppermint, eucalyptus, and lemon, no artificial chemicals, and 100% love. Developed by Shelley McVicar for her own horse as an alternative to toxic products, Shelley, a yoga instructor and lives a holistic lifestyle began selling Shelley Says to horse trainers and friends who swore about its efficacy...so the wait is over, Shelley Says! is now available to you.
