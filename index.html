About us

Using my background in essential oils, I created Shelley Says! Fly Away!, a non-toxic, all-natural insect repellent. It was originally designed so I could ride my horse without being attacked by flies. As a mother of three children, I quickly discovered it works great for a lot of outdoor activities such as spraying around a barbecue or on a picnic table. It’s non-toxic so you don’t have to worry inhaling the chemicals that are in many insect repellents on the market. It’s made with love and kindness from my heart to yours.
